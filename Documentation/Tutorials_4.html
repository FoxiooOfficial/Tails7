Tutorial 3: Changing Game Resolution


Here you can find out how to change the resolution of the game

Tails7 is designed to support any resolution. Changing the resolution itself is very easy.

The first step is to select our project and go to Properties, so there we change to the resolution we are most interested in. I changed from 1280x720 to 640x480




As you can see, the resolution has changed and everything is displayed correctly.




In addition, there are the variables _ResolutionX and _ResolutionY in the Tails7 Info Object. If you plan to target a specific one resolution then you can specify in these variables, but you can simply leave it at 1280x720.



If the target resolution is too small, things may be too big on the screen. That's why it's a good idea to double _ResolutionX and _ResolutionY (Example: from 640x480 it to 1280x960).
