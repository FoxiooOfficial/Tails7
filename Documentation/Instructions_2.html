Engine Limits and Known Issues


Currently known problems are the ones listed, I will fix it as soon as possible.

  1. Texture positioning problem with Tails7 Render Ground in Y axis at different resolutions as well as by _TextureSizeX and _TextureSizeY

  2. Problem with visibility of Fade Objects by too close camera positions

  3. Problem with visibility of Fade Objects by too large and small values of _Distortion

  4. Problem with visibility of Line Objects by too close camera positions

  5. Problem with visibility of Line Objects by too large and small values of _Distortion

  6. Android compatibility problem (Shader bugs)

  7. Gradient object scale dependent on actual texture size Tails7 Render Ground

  8. No _RotY support from shader for mapping
